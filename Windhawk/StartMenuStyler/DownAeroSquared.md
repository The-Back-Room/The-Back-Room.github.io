### Requirements

- [Windhawk](https://windhawk.net/) 
- [Windhawk](https://windhawk.net/) Plugin: [Windows 11 Start Menu Styler](https://windhawk.net/mods/windows-11-start-menu-styler)

- Click `Save`, then click `Load`
