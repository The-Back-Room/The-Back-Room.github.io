> ℹ️ This theme is designed for Windows 10 and 11. It includes wallpapers, color schemes, and sounds inspired by the popular anime and manga series Bleach. All content is sourced from various artists and is intended for personal use only.

---

<img src="/assets/images/preview-images/windows-themes/bleach.png" alt="Preview" width="100%" height="auto" />

---

<h3 align="center">Features</h3>

* **Multiple Wallpapers**: Multiple HD Wallpapers
* **Custom Change Theme Sound**: Bleach OST 3
* **Custom Cursors**: Windows 11 Concept Tailless Version by [jepriCreations](https:/deviantart.com/jepriCreations)
* **Alternate Wallpapers**: 26 alternate wallpapers for those who want them
